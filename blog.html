Future Law Legal Tech

November 2023

I was genuinely excited to have the opportunity to present at Legal Tech 2023 hosted at the prestigious Park Hyatt Hotel in Vienna.
The platform allowed me to showcase our company's newest product, XPERT Web, to a diverse and engaged audience comprising numerous professionals from various backgrounds. It was a rewarding experience sharing insights and perspectives on the evolving landscape of legal technology applications. The positive reception underscored the value of our innovation, and interacting with a broad spectrum of legal and tech industry individuals provided a valuable networking opportunity.
Beyond the formal presentation, the event facilitated insightful discussions and exchanges of ideas, fostering a collaborative atmosphere conducive to the advancement of innovative solutions in our dynamic field.
